\subsection{Detailed Description}
Runs Sequence\-Commands in sequential order. 

A variation on the Command\-Binder that runs Commands in sequence rather than in parallel. Each \hyperlink{classstrange_1_1extensions_1_1sequencer_1_1impl_1_1_sequence_command}{Sequence\-Command} in the Sequence Executes then terminates, and then the next \hyperlink{classstrange_1_1extensions_1_1sequencer_1_1impl_1_1_sequence_command}{Sequence\-Command} continues the process. Any \hyperlink{classstrange_1_1extensions_1_1sequencer_1_1impl_1_1_sequence_command}{Sequence\-Command} in the series is permitted to call {\ttfamily \hyperlink{classstrange_1_1extensions_1_1sequencer_1_1impl_1_1_sequencer_a3385c0c9de68e589eb70c7a617842498}{Break\-Sequence()}} to terminate the chain.
